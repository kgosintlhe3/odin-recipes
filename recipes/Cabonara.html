Cabonara Recipe

Lovely Creamy Cabonara

Easy to follow and healthy recipe,
cook a lovely meal for you and
your family in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Egg
  • Parmesan
  • Pepper spice
  • Olive oil(or any cooking oil)
  • Bacon
  • Mushrooms
  • Single Cream

Steps

  1. Place pasta in a pot of boiled water and let it simmer for 12 minutes
  2. Mix 1 eggs with 3 table spoons of parmesan in a bowl, then leave in the fridge
  3. Heat the pan with olive oil
  4. Chop mushrooms into medium sized bits
  5. Throw in the mushrooms and bacon in the hot oil and fry until brown
  6. Add in 2 spoons of pepper on top of the bacon and mushrooms
  7. take the pasta out from the boiling water and add it into the pan with the bacon and mushrooms
  8. Add in the mixed egg and parmesan into the pot
  9. Add in the half cup of single cream and stir until sauce is thick
