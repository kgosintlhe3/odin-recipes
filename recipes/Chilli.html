Spicy Chilli

Chilli for the Cold nights at home
With some Heat!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • 200g of minced beef
  • olive oil for cooking
  • 1 chilli pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Yellow pepper
  • Red kidney beans
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Chilli mixed spice
  • Olive oil(or any cooking oil)

Steps

  1. Boil water in kettle
  2. after kettle is boil add both water and rice in a pot and simmer for 12.
    (Rice measurements: For every 1 cup of rice, Add two cups of water.)
  3. Chop all the vegatables into small cubes
  4. Heat the pan with oil ready for frying
  5. Add in the onion first, fry until light brown
  6. Throw in the mince and fry until brown
  7. Add in the rest of the vegatables and red kidney beans
  8. Throw in the chilli spice and frying for 2 minutes
  9. Add in the chopped tomatoes with a little bit of water
  10. Let it simmer for 10 minutes, keep stirring threw out
  11. Dinner is Ready!
