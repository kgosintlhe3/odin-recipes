Juicy Steak and Chips

Simple steak and chips recipe to enjoy in the evening
or Date night in at home

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • 1 large potato
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil(or any cooking oil)
  • Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Pre Heat oven at 150c
  2. Season Steak with salt and pepper, let it side in room on the side for 5 minutes
  3. Chop potato into little wedges, then season with salt, pepper and cayenee for abit of spice
  4. Place potatos in oven for 20 minutes
  5. Heat the pan with olive oil
  6. Place steak on the heated oil and fry for 5 minutes each side
  7. After cooking steak place it in a tub for let it rest for further 5 minutes to drain the water out
  8. After 20 minutess take the potatos chips out and place them on plate with the Steak
  9. Dinner is served
